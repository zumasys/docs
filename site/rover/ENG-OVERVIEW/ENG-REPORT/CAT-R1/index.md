##  Category Listing (CAT.R1)

<PageHeader />

**Form Details**  
[ Form Details ](CAT-R1-1/index.md)   

**Purpose**  
The purpose of CAT.R1 is to display a listing of the categories that have been defined in the category entry procedure, [ CAT.CONTROL ](../../ENG-ENTRY/CAT-CONTROL/index.md) . 

**Frequency of Use**  
As required.

**Prerequisites**  
Categories must be entered in [ CAT.CONTROL ](../../ENG-ENTRY/CAT-CONTROL/index.md) . [ CAT.CONTROL ](../../ENG-ENTRY/CAT-CONTROL/index.md) can be found in the data entry section of the engineering module.
