# Resolving Random Screen Jumping and Unexpected Tab Openings in RoverERP

<PageHeader />

<badge text='Administration' vertical='middle' />

## Problem Statement

Users experience screens or tabs jumping randomly within RoverERP. For example, when attempting to add an attachment in a module, the expected screen opens, but another tab may unexpectedly open in the background on the previous window.

---

## Symptoms

- When performing actions such as adding attachments, additional tabs or screens open unexpectedly
- The application appears to jump between screens or open modules without user intent
- The issue is intermittent and may be difficult to reproduce consistently

---

## Cause

- This is a known issue in RoverERP, often triggered when users navigate or click rapidly within the program
- Rapid actions can cause the application to get out of sync, resulting in unexpected behavior such as random tab openings or screen jumps
- The program may not be able to recover from this out-of-sync state without user intervention

---

## Resolution Steps

### 1. Slow Down Navigation

- Avoid clicking rapidly or performing multiple actions in quick succession within RoverERP
- Allow each screen or action to fully load before proceeding to the next step

### 2. Close Unnecessary Tabs and Windows

- Periodically close unused tabs or windows within the application to reduce the chance of the program becoming out of sync

### 3. Restart RoverERP

- If the application becomes unresponsive or continues to behave unexpectedly, save your work and restart RoverERP to reset the session

---

## Verification

- [ ] After slowing down navigation and restarting the application, confirm that screens and tabs behave as expected
- [ ] Monitor for any recurrence of random tab openings or screen jumps

---

## Note

- This is a known issue and may be addressed in future RoverERP updates
- If the problem persists or severely impacts workflow, contact RoverERP support for further assistance

---

## Additional Information

- Always save your work frequently to prevent data loss if the application becomes unstable
- Report specific scenarios and steps to support to help with troubleshooting and future improvements
