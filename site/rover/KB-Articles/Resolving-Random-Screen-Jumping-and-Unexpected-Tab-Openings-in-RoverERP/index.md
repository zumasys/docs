# Resolving Random Screen Jumping and Unexpected Tab Openings in RoverERP

<PageHeader />

<badge text='Administration' vertical='middle' />

---

## Resolution Steps

### 1. Slow Down Navigation

- Avoid clicking rapidly or performing multiple actions in quick succession within RoverERP
- Allow each screen or action to fully load before proceeding to the next step

### 2. Close Unnecessary Tabs and Windows

- Periodically close unused tabs or windows within the application to reduce the chance of the program becoming out of sync

### 3. Restart RoverERP

- If the application becomes unresponsive or continues to behave unexpectedly, save your work and restart RoverERP to reset the session
